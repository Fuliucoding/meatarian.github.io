The Rise of Meatarians

An anti-veggie-meat organisation called the Meatarians was formed to retaliate against the oppressive ban of real meat products and to fight for the freedom of food consumption.

LIVECORE corporation is the leading company of meat plant production, manipulating the trajectory of society towards veggie meat and away from the real meat.

Meatarians launched a large-scale coup d'etat against LiveCore headquarters; up to 5000 members were involved. This led to several conflicts between Meatarians and LiveCore. However, this conflict ultimately ended with anonymous bombings of Meatarian bases and hideouts. With further assassinations of Meatarian leaders, many suspect that LiveCore was behind their downfall.

Origins: Meatarian.org was created to reunite all meat believers and to unravel the truth LiveCore has hidden from us.

The Impact: The remaining members of the Meatarians are still on the run, hiding among the broader fabric of society.

Looking Ahead

As we grow and expose more truths, our influence becomes an entity that Livecore cannot ignore. We must rise for justice.
